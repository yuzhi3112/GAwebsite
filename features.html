Service Features

Document Upload

Being able to upload a document of user photo that is being stored by the system. User photos include government-issued IDs, passports, and other official documents.

Real-Time Identity Verification

Using facial recognition to scan the user's face in real time to verify their identity before authorizing them access to their account.

Automatic Reporting

When the system detects any suspicious activity after analyzing user behavior, it alerts the user and sends a warning. A Suspicious Transaction Report (STR) will also be filed to relevant authorities like financial institutions (the bank) handling the account, as well as regulatory institutions such as MAS (Monetary Authority of Singapore). This helps address fraud prevention.

How does it address fraud prevention and anti-money laundering (AML) compliance

Fraud Prevention

AFter the document is uploaded and stored into the system , the system would be able to scan the user face in real time before authorizing them access into the account , thus even with identity theft and the attacker trying to access someone account , he/she would not be granted access , this helps prevent fraudulent transactions .

AML compliance

By allowing for document upload is also addressing aml compliance of customer due diligence as it is implementing EKYC (know your customer) by allowing the user to upload documents of themselves It Then helps the AI system to implement customer due diligence (cdd) to verify the identity of its clients, assess their suitability and any potential risks of illegal intentions by using a digital ID to authenticate and authorize various services.
